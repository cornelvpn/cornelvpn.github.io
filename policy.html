Privacy Notice for Cornel VPN

Welcome to Cornel VPN! At Cornel VPN, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Notice explains in detail how Cornel VPN ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the Cornel VPN application ("Cornel VPN") or interact with our services. Please read this notice carefully to understand our practices regarding your data and how we handle it.

  1. Information We Collect

    We collect various types of information to provide and improve the Cornel VPN services. This includes:

    Personal Information: When you sign up for Cornel VPN, we may collect your email address, username, and payment details (if applicable) to create and manage your account. This information is necessary to identify you as a user and process any subscription payments.

    Device and Usage Information: When you use Cornel VPN, we collect technical data such as your IP address, device type (e.g., mobile, tablet, or computer), operating system version, browser type, and unique device identifiers. We also collect usage data, including connection timestamps, the volume of data transferred, server locations you connect to, and logs of app activity (e.g., errors or crashes). This information helps us ensure the app works correctly and enhances your experience.

    Voluntary Information: If you contact our support team or provide feedback, we may collect additional details you choose to share, such as your name, contact information, or specific inquiries. This helps us assist you effectively.

    We strive to minimize the data we collect to only what is necessary for delivering Cornel VPN services.

  2. How We Use Your Information

    Cornel VPN uses the information we collect for the following purposes:

    Service Delivery: To provide you with the core functionality of Cornel VPN, such as establishing secure VPN connections, routing your traffic through our servers, and ensuring uninterrupted service.

    Improvement and Optimization: To analyze usage patterns, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve the performance, reliability, and features of Cornel VPN. For example, we may use crash logs to fix bugs or usage data to optimize server capacity.

    Account Management: To manage your account, process payments, send you service-related notifications (e.g., subscription renewals), and respond to your inquiries or support requests.

    Security: To enhance the security of Cornel VPN by detecting and preventing abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access to our systems. This includes monitoring for suspicious activity and maintaining the integrity of our network.

    Cornel VPN does not use your information for advertising purposes, nor do we sell or share it with third parties for their marketing benefit.

  3. Data Security

    Cornel VPN takes the security of your information seriously and employs robust measures to protect it. We use industry-standard encryption protocols (e.g., AES-256) to secure your data in transit between your device and our servers. Our servers are hosted in secure facilities with access controls and monitoring to prevent unauthorized entry.

    We also implement internal policies and technical safeguards, such as firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and regular security audits, to protect your data from loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach, Cornel VPN will notify affected users as required by applicable laws.

  4. Your Choices and Rights

    Cornel VPN empowers you to control your data through various options:

    App Settings: You can configure settings within Cornel VPN to manage how the app operates, such as selecting server locations or enabling features like a kill switch. These choices may influence the data we collect.

    Account Management: You can update your account information or delete your account by contacting us. Note that deleting your account may not erase all data immediately if we are required to retain it for legal purposes (see our retention policy below).

    Legal Rights: Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under data protection laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union. These rights include the ability to access a copy of your personal data, request corrections, restrict or object to processing, or demand deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below. We will respond within the timeframes required by law (typically 30 days).

    Opt-Out: You may opt out of non-essential communications, such as promotional emails, by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages or contacting us directly.

  5. Changes to This Notice

    Cornel VPN may modify this Privacy Notice from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal obligations, or the functionality of Cornel VPN. We will post the updated notice on this page and indicate the date of the last revision at the top. If we make material changes—such as altering how we collect or use your data—we will notify you through the Cornel VPN app, by email, or via another prominent method, as required by law.

    We encourage you to review this Privacy Notice periodically to stay informed about how we handle your information. Your continued use of Cornel VPN after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised notice.

Data Retention: Cornel VPN retains your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this notice or to comply with legal obligations. For example, account data is kept while your account is active and for a limited period afterward to address disputes or legal claims. Usage logs, if collected, are anonymized or deleted within a short timeframe (e.g., 30 days) unless required for security investigations or legal compliance.

Children’s Privacy: Cornel VPN is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete it immediately. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers: Cornel VPN operates globally, and your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our servers or service providers are located. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure that any international transfers comply with applicable regulations (e.g., GDPR) and use safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses to protect your data.
